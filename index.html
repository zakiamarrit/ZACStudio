The Role of Online Meetings during The Pandemic

COVID-19 has really changed all aspects of life.


One of them is a change in the application of meetings. In the past, meetings were often held offline in one place. However, with the covid 19, this has made more meetings held online to avoid exposure to this virus.


Some platforms that have skyrocketed in the present are Zoom, Google Meetings and Microsoft Teams. Each of these platforms has its own advantages, so it is important for us to determine which platform is the most suitable for each of our needs. Because everyone's needs are different. So, choose your suitable one!
